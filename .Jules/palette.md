## 2024-03-24 - Accessible Custom Radio Buttons
**Learning:** When building custom radio button options (`<div class="option">`), screen reader support and keyboard navigation are critical. A full custom implementation requires `role="radiogroup"` on the parent, `role="radio"`, `tabindex="0"`, and dynamic `aria-checked` on each option. Additionally, binding the 'Enter' and 'Space' keys for selection is crucial for parity with native inputs, keeping in mind to call `e.preventDefault()` on 'Space' to prevent the page from jumping/scrolling unexpectedly.
**Action:** Always implement full ARIA radiogroup/radio roles and manage keyboard selection + preventDefault logic when replacing native `<input type="radio">` with custom HTML tags. Ensure `querySelectorAll` is scoped to the specific component to maintain rendering performance.
