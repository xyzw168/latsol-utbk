## 2024-03-24 - Accessible Custom Radio Buttons
**Learning:** When using non-native interactive elements (like `<div>` elements styled as options) to serve as a group of radio buttons, keyboard accessibility requires a specific combination of ARIA attributes (`role="radiogroup"`, `role="radio"`, `aria-checked`), tabindex management (`tabindex="0"`), and custom keydown event listeners handling 'Enter' and 'Space' (using `e.preventDefault()` to stop page scrolling), paired with `:focus-visible` styles to give visual feedback without affecting mouse users.
**Action:** When creating custom selectable options, avoid native `<input type="radio">` constraints by applying this robust ARIA and keyboard handling pattern to maintain accessibility while allowing fully custom styling.
