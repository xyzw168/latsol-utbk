## 2026-05-02 - Custom Accessible Options\n**Learning:** Standard `div` elements acting as options are completely invisible to screen readers and keyboard users. Assigning roles (radiogroup/radio), managing aria-checked state, and ensuring they are focusable and clickable via Space/Enter drastically improves a11y.\n**Action:** Always ensure custom interactive elements have a role, are focusable (tabindex), reflect state (aria-checked/selected), and respond to standard keyboard interactions (Enter/Space).
